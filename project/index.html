We are a small group of astronomy enthusiasts located in the Gallatin County MT valley of Three Forks. We meet on the 3rd Tuesday of every month in the Three Forks Community Library at 6pm. Our outdoor viewing events are held monthly at the Bellach Park on Friday evenings closest to new moon date for the month. Come and see what we are all about. If you enjoy it please fill out our club sign-up form to officially become a member. It's free an you will receive a copy of our quarterly newsletter. Allan Cox: President
